Castle Wood Cottages

Live Your Fairytale

We've put everything into this cabin to make it a unique and romantic adventure. It includes a moat with waterfall, love swing for two, halls adorned with pewter and weaponry, a talking suit of armor, and adjustable, romantic lighting.

For dinner, head downstairs to find a king's banquet table, with hand-carved chairs from Italy. Up the spiral staircase inside the castle turret, and you will find a four poster carved bed, 2 of 3 stained glass arch windows, 14-foot high double arches, and a balcony.

The deck out front leads to your own private gazebo. A great place to sip your coffee in the morning, or perhaps to watch the snow fall.

You will feel like royalty in this 960 sq.ft. cabin. This is a place to forget the present, and transport yourselves back to the days of kings, queens, and dragons!
