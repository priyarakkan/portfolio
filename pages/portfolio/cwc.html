Castle Wood Cottages, Romantic Theme Cottages

Castle Wood ideal Southern California romantic getaway for two, a truly unique lodging experience located in a quiet setting near the Village of Big Bear Lake, just minutes away from the ski slopes and marinas.

Creative Brief

The Redesign
